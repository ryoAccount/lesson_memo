## 1. Do you agree that soft skills are more important than ever?

I think so, but I think we need both soft skills and hard skills more than ever.
I think soft skills don't replace generative AI and hard skills are needed to verify whether generative AI is correct or not.

## 2. How important would you say soft skills are in your line of work?

I think soft skills are as important as money.
We definitely need to work with someone, so soft skills are so helpful to communicate with someone.
People who are not good at communication might lose an opportunity to promote.

## 3. Are there any soft skills you'd like to develop or improve?

I want to improve my anger management skill.
I'm sometimes frustrated to someone when someone says their complaint and doesn't reply chat messages.

## 4. Have you ever worked with someone who had poor people skills?

Yes, I have ever worked with like that business person.
He took advantage of his anger to manage his team members.
However, he just followed his upper management kindly and friendly.
So he didn't get along with everyone in the office.

## 5. What skills do you think will be most valuable in the workplace in 20 years?

I think the creativity is the most important soft skill in 20 years.
New technologies were created in the past because of the creativity such as generative AI and Smartphone.
That means, new jobs and new occupations will be created by the creativity in the future from now on as well.
